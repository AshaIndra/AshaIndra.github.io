ABOUT ME

After years on the startup grind, I've packed my bags and headed out for the great unknown. Having honed my practice crafting growth marketing strategy for C-level San Francisco executives, I now work with select clients on a one-on-one basis. My passions lie in crafting brand strategy and customer acquisition programs that utilize all levels of the marketing stack.
